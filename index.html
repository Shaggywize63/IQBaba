Is IQ Baba free for students? +
Yes. Daily practice, streaks and the leaderboard are 100% free for every student. Premium gives you live mentor sessions and unlimited mock Olympiads, but you'll never lose access to the core habit.
Which boards and grades do you cover? +
CBSE, ICSE, IB and most state boards from Class 3 to Class 12. Our content is mapped to NCERT plus the standard Olympiad syllabi (SOF, ICAS, Silverzone, NSO and more).
How is this different from a textbook app? +
Textbook apps make you read. We make you do. Every five-minute drop is an active workout with adaptive difficulty, instant feedback and a tiny celebration at the end. It's closer to a game than a class.
Do my parents and school get visibility? +
Yes — parents get a calm weekly digest. Teachers get a class heatmap that shows exactly which concepts the class hasn't cracked yet. No screen-time guilt-trips, just useful signal.
Can I prepare for a specific Olympiad? +
Absolutely. Pick a target (e.g. IMO, NSO, NCO) and IQ Baba builds a 6 / 12 / 24 week plan. You'll get past papers, predicted papers and a final mock Olympiad three days before exam day.
My friend wants to join. How? +
Share your squad code from your dashboard. They sign up in 60 seconds, join your squad and you both get bonus XP for the first week.
